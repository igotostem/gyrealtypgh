Frequently Asked Questions

Below are common questions we are asked. Contact us if you have any questions.

What areas do you service?

We service a 30-mile radius centered around Pittsburgh. We can make special accommodations in certain cases. Contact us to discuss possible options.

What types of headstones can be cleaned?

We service most Granite and Marble stones. Bronze stones can also be cleaned. Most headstones can be cleaned by us. During the estimating process, we will determine the material and its ability to be cleaned.

I don't know the exact stone material; can you still clean it?

We will be able to determine the type during our FREE estimate. No worries if you don't know!

Why should I have the monument cleaned?

There are many reasons. Stains on the monument can become permanent over time. Cleaning the stone will remove mosses, mildew, and several other types of organic material from the stone which can cause these stains. Cleaning headstones can help to preserve the stone for years to come.

Will cleaning the stone damage it?

We will not damage the stone. We use specialized products and techniques to ensure a proper cleaning without sacrificing the quality of the stone.

What do you use to clean?

We use a combination of water, a specialized cleaning solution, and an assortment of purpose-made brushes. The solution is non-toxic, and safe for both the stone and the environment. It will not damage the stone.

Do you use a power washer?

No! Power washing a headstone is extremely dangerous. The lettering and engravings are often fragile and may break off. We only use hand tools - we use only the highest quality tools. We never recommend power washers.

Do you offer service for entire cemetaries?

Yes we do. For Cemetary inquiries, please email us at contact@stoneworkservices.com. We will be more than happy accommodate all needs.

I still have questions, who can I contact?

Contact us! Feel free to call (412) 449-9130 or use the form on our website.
