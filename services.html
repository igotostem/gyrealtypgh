Services We Offer

See below for a general list of our services. We will work with you to deliver exactly what you want. Contact us if you would like arrange a special order or would like to see a catalog of markers, flags, and flowers.

  • Contact Us to Schedule
Name Description Starting At
Premium Cleaning - Single or Annual Includes the cleaning of the stone and trimming of debris surrounding the base. We use our special product to help to remove all of the matter on the stone. We use special brushes to ensure no damage. The stone will typically stay clean for months to years. Contact Us
Marker and Flag We offer markers which accept a 12”x18” flag. Choose from over 50 variations made from high quality bronze and aluminum. Our selection ranges from Veteran to Public Service. American Flag is included. $45*
Stick Flag Decoration We have various sized flags which are placed in ground using our in-house holder. We offer 4"x6", 8"x10", and 12"x18" flags. Choose from various styles. $5*
Flower Decoration Choice of flower placed at site. Can choose in ground plants (per cemetery rules), or bouquet style flowers. Live or imitation flowers are available. $55*
Veteran Special We provide the full cleaning service. We will restore the stone using our special system. In addition, we will place an American flag with our in-house holder completely free of charge. Choose size. Contact Us
Other Monument Services We can service entire cemetaries. In addition, we service various types of monuments, from grave to statue. Contact to make an arrangement. Contact Us
Photo Delivery We will send pictures of the finished site to the provided email. Always free of charge! Included

Please be aware that exact pricing will be provided at time of estimate. The size, location, and design of the stone affect pricing.

*Marker, flag, and flower prices listed are for addition to cleaning service.
