The Process

We have written out our process of cleaning the stone. We take extreme pride in providing top-quality cleaning and services. If you have any questions about the process, feel free to contact us.

The process starts by working with you to determine exactly what you want. We will provide an estimate after visiting the stone site. We will then discuss the exact service which we will provide.

Upon arrival in the cemetery, we will carefully remove overgrown grass around the base of the stone. We will brush off any lose debris, such as dust, grass, and leaves.

After the preparation stages, we begin to clean the stone. We use a special product which is formulated especially for monuments. This product is non-toxic, biodegradable, and is safe around both the stone and wildlife. We never use bleaches, ammonia, acids, or other harsh chemicals which can wear the stone and damage it.

We then use an assortment of brushes to scrub the entire stone. We have special brushes which allow us to get into the engraving of the stone without damaging the fragile lettering. Every letter and design are carefully cleaned.

After the actual cleaning, we will rinse the entire stone. We will then use a different cleaning product to wipe the polished surfaces of the stone to reveal a nice shiny surface.

We take extreme caution through the entire process. We pride ourselves in taking the proper amount of time to ensure both a quality clean without damage to the stone. We guarantee your satisfaction!

WE NEVER USE POWERWASHERS! Using powerwashers often damages the stone due to the fragile structure of lettering and other engravings. We only use manual hand tools to clean the stones in order to protect them and fully restore them.
