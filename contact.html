About Us

The owner of GY Realty LLC, Bryce, has been working in the real estate industry for years. He started with a landscaping company before he found his true passion in real estate.

GY Realty LLC was started when Bryce noticed that many real estate investors cut corners and did improper work, resulting in unhappy buyers. Bryce vowed to do the right thing by creating quality renovations up to codes and standards.

GY Realty LLC renovates houses to modern styles which new homeowners love! Our properties use high quality materials - we don't source materials from the clearance section at the home improvement store. Instead, we use long lasting materials to ensure homebuyers have a long lasting, positive experience!

Our Mission

We provide high quality, move-in ready houses. Our goal is to create the house you love! We take pride in our properties, ensuring that our quality renovations are done properly and by current standards.
